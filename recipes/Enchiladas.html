Cheesy Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • Grilled Chicken
  • Enchilada Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Tortillas

Instructions

  1. Boil Chicken until fully cooked through
  2. Shred the chicken once cooked
  3. Wrap shredded chicken in the tortillas and add to a baking pan
  4. Cover the enchiladas in sauce and cheese
  5. Cook in the oven until cheese is melted and everything is hot
