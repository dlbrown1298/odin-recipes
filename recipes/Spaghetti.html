Amazing Spaghetti

Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ragu Sauce
  • Parmesean Cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil Spaghetti for 7 minutes
  2. Strain the water from the coooked Spaghetti
  3. Return Spaghetti to the pot
  4. Add Ragu sauce to the Spaghetti
  5. Top with Parmesean cheese and Enjoy!
