Grilled Chicken Burrito

Ingredients

  • Grilled Chicken
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Spanish Rice

Instructions

  1. Toast the bell peppers and onions on medium heat until caramelized
  2. Once done, add chciken and cooked until done
  3. Use microwaveable rice
  4. Warm tortillas up in microwave
  5. Wrap everything together and Enjoy!
